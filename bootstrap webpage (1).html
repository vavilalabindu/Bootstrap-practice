Cappuccino

As the name suggests, Simply South serves South Indian cuisine. It is one of the most popular restaurants in the city. That's mainly because of the amazing taste of the food served and the cozy and comfortable ambiance. They have 2 outlets in the city, both open for lunch and dinner. Food lovers adore this establishment for its signature South Indian Thali. You can choose from the option of having either Andhra style Thali, or Telangana style Thali, or Kerela style Thali, or Mangalorean style Thali. Each Thali would have Authentic food items from their respective origins and each and every one of those is mouth-watering! Apart from Thalis, you can also choose their A'la Carte menu, which is also very extensive and the dishes taste really good.
